Research Interests

  • Video analysis: detection, segmentation and tracking; trajectory prediction, pose estimation and behaviour analysis; video anomaly detection and localization
  • Multimedia Computing: multimodal content analysis for online safety, detecting and understanding harmful content online
  • Machine learning: self-supervised learning, transfer learning, incremental learning, multi-modal learning
  • Medical image/video analysis: ultrasound, neuro-facial image analysis; image/video segmentation; and human motion analysis for health monitoring
  • Satellite and drone imagery analysis: early detection of disasters and monitoring environmental changes
  • AI for Science: computational social science, biomedicine, engineering, and environment
